Hi,

Thank you for visiting my page.

I am a graduate student at Institute of Life Sciences, India working in the area of Computational Biology. My PhD research topic is epigenetic regulation of immune response in dendritic cells. Corepressors protein plays an important role in dynamic epigenetic modifications such as acetylation and deacetylation of chromatin that regulates gene expression. I am investigating role of these corepressors protein in regulating multiple immmune response upon dendritic cells activation.

I mainly use high througputput sequencing data such as RNASeq and ChIPseq to understand epigenetic regulation.

I feel really great working in this area of research that provides an immense opportunity to always learn something new from other disciplines.

I am also part of eLife Ambassadors Programmne to promote open science and reproducibility.
